    About Me

    Hello! My name is Lucy Kim and I'm a Freelance Web Developer based in Cary, North Carolina. I'm currently a student at Wake Technical Community College in the Web Development program. To give you a little more info about me, I originally started my career in customer service and I just knew it wasn't the right path for me. I took up UX Design, then switched over to Web Development because I found myself enjoying the coding aspect more even though it can be quite frustrating at times. Fast forward two years later and I'm set to graduate next week. It feels unreal if I'm being honest but I'm glad I didn't give up.


    If I'm not coding or studying, I'm usually playing games or cooking. Right now, I currently have a fixation on Stardew Valley. It's really fun and stressful. Oddly enough, it kinda reminds me of coding. But enough about my personal life, let's get into what you came here for. Currently, I offer web design services, both front-end and back-end. Check out the services page to get a feel for what I offer and check out my portfolio too! You might find something you like. And please don't hesitate to contact me for additional information and we can discuss the best options for you! See you soon!
